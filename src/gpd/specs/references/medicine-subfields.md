# Medical Research Subfield References

Comprehensive guidance for working within specific medical research subfields. Each subfield reference provides: key methods, standard tools, validation strategies, common pitfalls, and recommended software.

<purpose>
This reference is loaded by GMD agents when research involves a specific medical subfield. It provides:

1. **For the planner:** Which methods and tools are standard for the subfield, what validation checks to include in plans
2. **For the executor:** Correct standards, reporting guidelines, typical statistical parameters
3. **For the verifier:** Subfield-specific validation strategies, known benchmarks, reporting checklists
4. **For the researcher:** What databases, tools, and guidelines to consult
</purpose>

## Subfield Selection Guide

| If the research involves... | Primary subfield | Also consult |
|---|---|---|
| Systematic review or meta-analysis | Evidence Synthesis | Statistics domain verification |
| Randomized controlled trial | Clinical Trials | Epidemiology for design |
| Observational cohort/case-control | Epidemiology | Evidence Synthesis for bias assessment |
| Thermal/mineral water interventions | Balneology & Thermalism | Physical Medicine for rehabilitation outcomes |
| Cost-effectiveness or budget impact | Health Economics | Evidence Synthesis for effectiveness data |
| Aging, frailty, longevity | Gerontology | Internal Medicine for comorbidities |
| Drug efficacy or safety | Pharmacology | Clinical Trials for study design |
| Cancer treatment outcomes | Oncology | Clinical Trials + Evidence Synthesis |
